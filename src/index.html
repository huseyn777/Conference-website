Speakers

Ozcan
Ozturk

ozturk@cs.bilkent.edu.tr
Professor at Bilkent

Dr. Ozturk has been on the faculty at Bilkent since 2008 where he currently is a Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering. His research interests are in the areas of cloud computing, GPU computing, manycore accelerators, on-chip multiprocessing, computer architecture, heterogeneous architectures, and compiler optimizations.

Ibrahim
Korpeoglu

korpe@cs.bilkent.edu.tr
Professor at Bilkent

Ibrahim Korpeoglu received his Ph.D. and M.S. in Computer Science from University of Maryland at College Park in 2000 and 1996, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Satish Tripathi. He received his B.S. degree (summa cum laude) in Computer Engineering, from Bilkent University in 1994. Since 2002, he is a faculty member in the Department of Computer Engineering of Bilkent University.
